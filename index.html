I was born on June 26, 2004 in Buenos Aires, Argentina, but my family moved to Entre Ríos, where I studied at the Technical Education School No. 2 "Francisco Ramírez," where my passion for programming was born. I started programming on my own at the age of 13, initially creating video games. Over time, I delved into logical programming with Java, which developed my logical thinking as a programmer.

Aprendí Programación Orientada a Objetos y diversas áreas como la programación web, donde conocí HTML, CSS y JavaScript, y más tarde aprendí tecnologías como NodeJS y ReactJS para desarrollar aplicaciones Full-Stack. I learned Object-Oriented Programming and various areas such as web programming, where I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and later I learned technologies like NodeJS and ReactJS to develop Full-Stack applications. Over the past 7 years, I have explored niches like Python, Artificial Intelligence, C++, and three-dimensional graphics programming with OpenGL and Vulkan.
